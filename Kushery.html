The Kushery website

Built with react.js

When I was in collage I posted a craigslist ad to see if any company needed a website built so I could get some experience, a local Cannabis shop reached out to me and I built them this website. Shortly after I finished, they asked if I wanted to take care of their websites and google accounts and I accepted the job offer. I designed, developed, and deployed this webpage, all in react.js, deployed and set up SSL with Microsoft Azure. My next steps for this webpage are making design improvements, and implementing SASS for the better styling organization.
